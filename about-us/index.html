Our History

Transformation Place was founded in 2018 as Church of His Presence East London. Services were held at Park Hotel in Ilford

We moved to many different locations, but ended up staying at Forest Gate Community School.

In the beginning of 2025, we renamed the church to Transformation Place, and moved are currently looking for a new venue..
